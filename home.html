About Me

My name is Yvonne and I am working towards becoming a Library Media Specialist. In my free time, I love to spend time with family, read books, and go camping.
My favorite genres of books to read are mystery and dystopia.

Teaching Background

I just completed my 16th year of teaching. In my teaching career, I have taught fourth grade and Kindergarten.
Reading has always been my favorite subject to teach, and I have always had a small "library" in my classroom.
Instilling a love of reading in students has been one of my greatest joys in teaching. My love for books and libraries sparked my interest in becoming a librarian.
I have always enjoyed helping students choose interesting books, form literature circles in the classroom, guide students through book reports,
and let students act out stories. It is my hope as a librarian that I can guide students in choosing books for research projects and to read for enjoyment.
